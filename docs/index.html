Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2025

Welcome to H_News. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use the H_News Android application.

1. Data Collection

H_News does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. The application functions exclusively as a reader for publicly available content retrieved from the official Hacker News API, maintained by Y Combinator.

2. Permissions

The app does not request or require any special permissions on your device. It functions entirely through public API access and does not interact with local storage, contacts, location data, or any other device-level resources.

3. Third-Party Services

H_News uses the official Hacker News API to retrieve news content. We do not control, modify, or take responsibility for the content served through the API. You are advised to review Hacker News’s own policies for more information about their data practices.

4. Children’s Privacy

The application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the updated version will be posted here with the revised effective date. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through GitHub or through the support email provided on the Google Play Store listing.
